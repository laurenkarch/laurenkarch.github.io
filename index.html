Portfolio of Work from MAPS 671 and 672

New Maps Plus student based in Ohio, hoping to master Leaflet and a natural-sounding "y'all."

Mapping Police Pursuits

Maps traffic fatalities involving police pursuits, using NHTSA data, and suggests mapping as a tool to further study pursuit-related fatalities. Map created QGIS 3.22.5. The basemap uses OpenStreetMap and Census TIGER data. Poster created in Adobe Illustrator.

Power Plant Sources

Interactive Leaflet map showing power source for power plants across the United States.

Median Incomes of Ohio School Districts

Static chloropleth map showing median household incomes of Ohio's school districts. Map created using Leaflet. Data from the Ohio Department of Education's 2018 District Profile Report.

Concentration of Wind Turbines

Static hexbinned map showing concentration of wind turbines in the United States. Data from United States Wind Turbine Database.

Troy Ohio Road Closures (Contributor)

Rudimentary ArcGIS StoryMap allowing collaboration across City departments, and accessible to residents.
